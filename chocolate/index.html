Welcome to the World of Milka

Milka chocolate is known for its creamy texture and rich flavor, made with 100% Alpine milk. Discover the variety of Milka, from its classic milk chocolate bars to inventive creations filled with nuts, fruit, and caramel.

The Story of Milka

Founded in 1901, Milka has been delivering quality Swiss chocolate to the world for over a century. The name Milka is derived from 'Milch' and 'Kakao,' the German words for milk and cocoa, the main ingredients of the chocolate.
